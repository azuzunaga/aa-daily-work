The Banging Bernardino Burger

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Burger served with lots of veggies, on a potato roll, with sweet potato fries.

Wikipedia

Ingredients

  • Potato roll
  • Burger patty
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Sweet potato fries

Directions

  1. Throw burger on a grill
  2. Put fries in fryer
  3. While burger is cooking chop your veggies into slices
  4. Flip burger and add cheese on top
  5. Place roll on grill when burger is just about ready
  6. Stack ingredients and burger patty on roll and place mayo on the other roll
  7. Serve with sweet potato fries
